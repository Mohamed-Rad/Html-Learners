The 1st question:
What is meant by HTML word?
Ans: HTML or (Hyper Text Markup Language) is a programming language used in making websites,games and more of codeable applications.


The 2nd question:
Does HTML works with codes?
Ans: Yes, It does for example:({p} , {br} , {b},.... etc) And now we will learn this codes and how to use it.







Now we will divide codes into two parts:


Main Codes                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Secondary Codes





Lets begin with:
Main Codes




But what are main codes?
Main Codes are the codes that show the structure of the website or how it will be written.

This codes are:






1. {html} and {/html}:      
Those two codes are used for the website to know that the website is written in html


2. {head} and {/head}:
Those two codes contains the head part of the webpage


3. {title} and {/title}:
Those two codes where the webpage title is written and it is written between {head} and {/head}


4. {body} and {/body}:
Those two codes where the webpage content is written and it is written between {body} and {/body}
